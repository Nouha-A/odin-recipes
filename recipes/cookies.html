Description

This recipe will yield: 4 Dozen cookies. ps. for half the amount , halve all the Ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 320 g flour
  • 280 g sugar
  • 160 g butter
  • 2 eggs
  • half tsp baking soda
  • pinch of slat
  • vanilla extract
  • 180g chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
  3. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips (you can add walnuts or anything you like)
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown. ps In an oven preheated to 350 degrees F, the chocolate chip cookies should be perfectly baked in about 10 minutes
  6. Store the cooled chocolate chip cookies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week. If you want to go the extra mile, throw a piece of white bread into the container â€” it'll absorb the dry air and keep the cookies soft for longer.
