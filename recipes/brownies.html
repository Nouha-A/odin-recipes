Brownies

Description

This recipe will yield: 4 Dozen Brownies, approximately 2 in. (5 cm) each. ps. for half the amount , halve all the Ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Coffee extract (optional) 15 ml
  • pastry or all-purpose flour 480 g

  • Powdered sugar or cocoa powder (optional) as needed
